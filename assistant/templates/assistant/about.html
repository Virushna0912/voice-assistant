Our Story

This project was developed by two members, Virushna Tharagan and Vishwaskaran, under the guidance of Mrs. K. GEETHA, M.Sc., M.Phil., B.Ed. and Dr.M.SUGANTHI,MCA.,M.Phil.,Ph.D. at KONGUNADU ARTS AND SCIENCE COLLEGE , COIMBATORE. The aim of the project is to develop a voice assistant that can be used by anyone worldwide to access desktop and web applications using voice commands.

V

Our Vision

To improve the accessibility of technology for people with disabilities and to make technology more user-friendly.

M

Our Mission

To deliver a voice assitant that can be used by anyone worldwide to access desktop and web applications using voice commands.

A

Our Aim

Innovation, excellence, integrity, and collaboration form the foundation of everything we do.
